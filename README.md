# Fred's Archive

A personal digital archive showcasing my creations across different fields:
programming, art, music, photography, and creative experiments.

This project exists as a place to collect, organize, and share the things I create throughout my journey.

---

## 🌱 About

Fred's Archive is a personal hub where different forms of creativity come together.

From coding projects and data analysis to drawings, photography, and music compositions, this website serves as a collection of my work and progress over time.

Rather than keeping my creations scattered across different folders and platforms, this archive brings everything together in one place.

---

## ✨ Features (V1)

- 🎨 Artwork gallery
  - Collection of drawings and visual creations

- 🎵 Music showcase
  - Original compositions and musical experiments

- 📷 Photography gallery
  - A collection of captured moments and photography work

- 💻 Programming projects
  - Showcasing software projects, experiments, and applications

- 👤 Personal introduction
  - A small section about my creative journey
